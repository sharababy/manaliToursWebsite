About Manali Leh MotorBike Tour
The adventure of riding your bike through Ladakh calls for tourists from around the globe.
The highlight of this tour is riding through the tough roads and High Mountain passes on your bike. Experience Ladakhi culture, a visit to Khardung La Pass, the highest motorable pass in the world along with visit to Pangong Tso, one of the most famous and breathtaking lakes in the world.

Don't Forget
  • Bring your valid drivers license as that would be the first thing you would have to produce
  • Consult your doctor for necessary precautions as this tour would be taking you on high passes (The tour is designed such that you get properly acclimatized while touring, to the high altitude conditions of the region)
  • Rain gear, suitable clothing to ride in cold conditions which protects you in an unlikely spill and from cold winds.
  • Protective bike touring jackets, trousers, gloves, set of boots, day sack, flip flops, Balaclava, sunscream(30 to 50 spf), loads of underwear and socks. Weather is unpredictable changes from hot sunbath to freezing within few kms ride
  • Medication if any- carry them from home
  • Finish all your work before you come for this tour as you would not find the time or the facilities to do so. We are going to places where 80% of the time there is no internet or cellular phone network. Call you near and dear ones from Manali or before
